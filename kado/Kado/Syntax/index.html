Module Kado.Syntax

The abstract syntax of the restricted predicate logic of cofibrations.

type ('r, 'a) endo =
  1. | Le of 'r * 'r
  2. | Join of 'a list
  3. | Meet of 'a list

A family of polynomial endofunctors ('r, -) t indexed in an interpretation of the interval algebra 'r. This is for multiple types (for example, abstract syntax) to embed the langauge of cofibrations.

type ('r, 'v) free =
  1. | Cof of ('r, ('r, 'v) free) endo
  2. | Var of 'v

For each interval algebra 'r, we define the free monad ('r, -) free on the polynomial endofunctor ('r, -) endo: each ('r, 'v) t is the language of cofibrations over an interval algebra 'r, with indeterminates drawn from 'v.
